About the Commission

The Hero Public Safety Commission serves as the primary governing body overseeing all professional hero activities in Japan. Established in the early days of quirk emergence, our mission is to maintain public order, regulate hero operations, and ensure the safety of all citizens in a world where 80% of the population possesses unique abilities.

Through strategic coordination, rigorous licensing standards, and comprehensive oversight programs, we work to uphold the integrity of the hero system while adapting to evolving threats against society.
