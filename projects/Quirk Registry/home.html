Registry Functions

  • Track quirk manifestations and hereditary patterns
  • Analyze quirk evolution and mutation rates
  • Support public safety and emergency response
  • Maintain comprehensive quirk classification system
  • Provide data for hero training programs

Quirk Classifications

  • Emitter: Allows user to generate and control substances or abilities
  • Transformation: Enables physical body alteration
  • Mutant: Permanent physical abnormality or enhancement
  • Accumulation: Stockpiles power or energy over time
  • Composite: Multiple quirk characteristics
