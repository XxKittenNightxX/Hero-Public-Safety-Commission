Description

The user manifests an "Objective Oracle," a mental voice that provides strategic guidance. When the user sets a specific goal and identifies a target, the Oracle offers the most effective path to achieve that objective. The Oracle's knowledge isn't universal, but it's omniscient within the context of the user's goals, delivering detailed insights and strategic advice.

Capabilities

  • Strategic Analysis: Provides optimal tactical approaches to achieving defined objectives
  • Goal-Oriented Omniscience: Knowledge is contextually infinite within the scope of the user's stated goals
  • Real-Time Guidance: Delivers continuous strategic advice during active operations
  • Target Assessment: Analyzes targets and provides comprehensive tactical data

Limitations & Drawbacks

  • Goal Dependency: Oracle only functions when a specific goal is clearly defined
  • Limited Scope: Knowledge is not universal - only pertains to active objectives
  • Mental Strain: Extended use can cause psychological fatigue
  • Requires goal: Must have a clearly identified goal for optimal guidance

Hereditary Analysis

Selene's quirk "Objective Oracle" is a unique mutation resulting from the combination of her parents' quirks. Genetic analysis indicates a complex synthesis that merged strategic and perceptive abilities.

Mother's Quirk: Strategic Mind

Quirk Type: Emitter

The ability to rapidly analyze tactical situations and formulate multi-step plans. Enhanced cognitive processing when evaluating strategic options and predicting outcomes based on current information.

Father's Quirk: Clairvoyance

Quirk Type: Emitter

Limited precognitive ability that provides glimpses of potential futures and hidden information related to specific targets or objectives. Information appears as intuitive insights rather than direct visions.

?? Quirk Synthesis

Objective Oracle synthesizes the strategic analysis capabilities of Strategic Mind with the information-gathering aspects of Clairvoyance. The result is a manifestation that provides goal-oriented omniscience— combining her mother's tactical processing with her father's intuitive knowledge acquisition. Unlike simple precognition, the Oracle delivers comprehensive strategic guidance by merging analytical and perceptive elements into a singular, purpose-driven advisory system.
