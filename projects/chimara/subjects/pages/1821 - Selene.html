Quirk Name
Quirk Type

The user manifests an "Objective Oracle," a mental voice that provides strategic guidance. When the user sets a specific goal and identifies a target, the Oracle offers the most effective path to achieve that objective. The Oracle's knowledge isn't universal, but it's omniscient within the context of the user's goals, delivering detailed insights and strategic advice.

Abilities

Strategic Analysis

The Oracle can assess complex situations, identify potential obstacles, and predict the outcomes of different courses of action.

Goal-Oriented Guidance

The Oracle provides clear, actionable strategies tailored to the user's goals, suggesting the most efficient and effective methods to achieve the desired outcome.

Targeted Knowledge:

The Oracle's knowledge is vast and precise, but it is limited to information relevant to the user's targets and goals, providing specific and useful advice.
