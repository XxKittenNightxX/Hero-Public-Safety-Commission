Quirk Information

Quirk Name
Quirk Type

Her unique Quirk enabled her to create human beings. These were typically quirkless, but if she made a person while touching another, the new person would inherit a heavily modified version of the other's Quirk. However, this ability had a strict limitation: she could only create one person for each person she touched in this way. To create more individuals, she would need to find new people to touch.

Abilities

Quirk Weaver

When using Progenitor to create a person while touching another, she doesn't just pass on a modified Quirk; she can subtly influence the nature of the inherited Quirk. She can, to a limited extent, choose which aspects of the original Quirk are amplified or suppressed. This requires a deep understanding of the original Quirk, allowing her to tailor the new person's abilities for specific purposes. This ability is limited by her understanding of the original Quirk and the number of people she has touched.

Echo Creation

When using Progenitor and has created a person with a modified Quirk, she can, at a later time, create a weaker "echo" of that person. These echoes are not fully human, but rather constructs of solidified energy that possess a fraction of the original's Quirk. The number of echoes she can create depends on her stamina and the strength of the original creation. These echoes can serve as scouts, distractions, or even temporary allies.

Genesis Burst

When using Progenitor she can channel her creation ability into a short-range burst of energy. This burst can rapidly accelerate the growth of organic matter. Imagine her touching a wilting flower and making it bloom instantly, or even accelerating the healing of minor wounds on herself or others. The burst's intensity and range depend on her current physical condition and the amount of energy she invests.
