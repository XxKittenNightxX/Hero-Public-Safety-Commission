Akako , can create stable, two-way portals between any two locations she has memorized the precise coordinates of. These portals don't just open; they rip a shimmering tear in the fabric of reality, revealing a swirling, breathtaking galaxy within. The size of the portal is initially limited to a small circle, but with practice and training, Akako can expand the portal's diameter to a maximum of 5 meters.

Abilities

Portal Generation

can instantly generate a portal between two pre-determined locations. The portals manifest as a tear in the sky, revealing a mesmerizing galaxy within the opening.

Coordinate Memory

possesses an exceptional memory for spatial coordinates. She can memorize and recall vast amounts of coordinate data.

Targeted Travel

The portals allow for instantaneous travel between the specified locations, allowing for strategic movement and surprise attacks.
