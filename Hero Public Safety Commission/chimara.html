Chimara Project

Historic program in partnership with MHA focusing on community resilience and long-term mental health supports.

Director
Red Miller
Start date
January 1, 1976
Status
Active
Overview
The Chimara Project is an innovative initiative designed to train and empower youth to become formidable heroes. This program aims to ensure that there is always a new generation of protectors ready to step in whenever an active hero retires or, unfortunately, passes away.
