Director Reports

Official assessments and observations from project directors and commission supervisors.

Report #1 — Project integration

Subject was a newborn with no observable Quirk abilities at birth, and displayed a high level of intelligence and adaptability from an early age. Though it is expected that the subject will manifest a powerful quirk in the future.

Report #2 — Lack of observable Quirk

Subject has not exhibited any observable Quirk abilities to date, despite thorough monitoring and testing. Continued observation is recommended to track potential future development. Though the subject's intelligence and adaptability continue to be more and more prominent.

Report #3 — Director observations and interest

Scientists started reporting that the subject seems to "see right through them" and discover all aspects of their behavior and intentions and quirk details, including methods to eliminate a scientist's quirk. This caused some of the scientists to fear the subject's potential capabilities, causing the director to take charge of the subject and introduce them to the special containment zone.

Report #4 — Director follow-up

The Director has chosen to hone the subject's quirk analysis abilities and intelligence to a higher level, ensuring the subject can effectively assess and understand Quirks in various scenarios. Continued monitoring and training are recommended to maximize the subject's potential.

Report #5 — Combat and Quirk analysis training

Subject has undergone initial combat training exercises, focusing on stealth, agility, and strategic thinking. Observations indicate a high level of adaptability and quick learning, suggesting potential for advanced combat proficiency. Continued training and assessment are recommended to further develop these skills. Additionally, the subject's quirk analysis abilities have been integrated into training exercises to enhance strategic decision-making and combat effectiveness, with the subject reviewing tapes of heroes and villains to determine strategies and weaknesses.

Report #6 — Practical training

The subject has started sparring with other subjects and has dominated the whole match, demonstrating superior combat skills and strategic thinking compared to the other subjects, and ended up permanently disabling the ability of some other subjects to use their quirks by physically damaging their bodies, causing their opponents to rapidly lose their rank until they were killed in a sparring match.

Report #7 — Quirk manifestation

The subject has manifested a quirk during training, and the subject ended up falling and disappearing. Further testing made us deem the quirk name "Shadow Synthesis" for their ability to manipulate shadows.
